Portal Knihovny.cz provides the unified access to services offered by Czech libraries - for everyone, from everywhere and anytime. This portal allows you to search catalogs of all participating libraries using a single search bar.

The densest public libraries infrastructure in the European Union is the pride of the Czech Republic, and Knihovny.cz has the ambition to become the gateway to the most of them (you can see the list of already participating libraries here).

In future, besides connecting even more libraries than now, we are planning to extend our portal by central index which will help you to search a varied range of quality worldwide expert resources. Portal Knihovny.cz could become your everyday tool for finding reliable informationand a go-between for obtaining it. Did not find what you were looking for in your library? Do you need to draw on trusted resources? Or are you just looking for a reader's inspiration? Then Knihovny.cz is just for you.

Knihovny.cz - Current Features

All participating libraries:

We pursue the goal to offer you in our portal the same features those available in individual systems of participating libraries, therefore:

  • Search publications in libraries
  • Making reservations of borrowed books and ordering publications from stack, loans renewing etc.
  • Overview of your loans, charges and library cards validity

Some of the participating libraries allow you to:

  • Make an on-line registration, pre-registration or remote registration (read more)
  • Pay your charges and fines
  • Search fulltext electronic resources of the library
  • Downloading and other options of using documents from electronic information sources

Additional features:

  • Linking identities from participating libraries where the reader is already registered
  • Advantages brought by linked accounts, as comfortable access to services of numerous libraries using unified interface
  • Tools for managing favorite titles, saving frequently used search queries ,RSS feeds...
  • Citations in various formats
  • Following book trends, inspiration for readers, user comments , availability of publications in e-shops...
  • Searchable address book of all Czech libraries
  • Constantly extending opportunities to access non-library resources
  • Central index of "International resources", which will help you to search a range of quality scientific journals, specializeddatabases and more electronic resources worldwide

Coming Soon:

  • Interlibrary loan service to get publications from other libraries; solution of this service is within the competence of the National Library of Technology
  • Electronic document delivery, enabling to order one-time or regular delivery of articles or other documents in either electronic or printed form; solution of this service is within the competence of the National Library of Technology
  • Connecting more libraries - the main goal of this project is to establish a portal for unified access to services of the majority of institutions belonging to the dense library network in the Czech Republic - the more connected libraries, the better the usability of the portal
  • We are constantly working on optimizing search functions, improving the design to provide more comfortable user experience and developing new features…

Security Policy

In the development of this portal, a great emphasis was put on the data security. This portal does not collect any information about users, their borrowings or their financial transactions. For signing in, it verifies the identity of the user through the library which is used for logging in. For secure sign-in etc. the security of websites and sing-in on the side of connected libraries is crucial. Communication between the user and portal launched by signing-in is set on the secure protocol https, and therefore it is protected from being abused.

Context

Portal Knihovny.cz is a project set in the Concept of Czech Republic Library Development for the years 2011 to 2015 and Concept of Czech Republic Library Development for the years 2017 to 2020. Organizational matters of the portal are the responsibility of SDRUK (Association of Czech Republic Libraries). The Central Portal of Libraries brings the entire Czech librarianship closer to trend , which is widespreadin Western Europe. Whether this concerns Finnish portal Finna.fi, Swissbib.ch, Danish Bibliotek.dk or Dutch Bibliotheek.nl. all these projects share a common goal to simplify accessibility of information and documents and offer the resources of the libraries including their services, all in one place.

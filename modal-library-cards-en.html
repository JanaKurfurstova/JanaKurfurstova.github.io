Link your identities from connected libraries and social networks.

Click on "Connect another identity" button and continue by logging in with an identity of any institution listed in "Connect with library identity" or social network identity from "Alternative login" list (Google, Facebook, LinkedIn, GitHub, ORCID).

Once you attach other connected library's identity to your account(*), you gain access an overview of your activities in this library, and you will be able to order or reserve publications.

In case you are not registered in any of the connected libraries and logged in just using a social network identity or ORCID, you will not be able to order or reserve a publication in connected libraries, though you can still save your favorite searches and records.

Learn more about registration in participating libraries here.

You can use any of your connected identities to access your account. You can simply disconnect your linked identities anytime. It is also possible to "Delete portal account" - this action removes all your user settings, saved searches and favorites lists. Disconnecting an identity or deleting a portal account affects only portal Knihovny.cz - it will not cancel your registration in any library.

Linking more than one identity within one library is not possible. It is also not possible to link one identity from library A with more than one identity from library B (as library cards are not transferable).

(*)The account in Knihovny.cz ties your linked library and social network identities. When you sing in for the first time, your portal account includes just one identity - that one you used for your first logging in. The decision to connect more identities is up to you.
SAFETY WARNING: To protect your privacy we strongly dicourage you from linking identities on public or shared computers!


We strongly recommend to link identities on your own computer.

In case you really need to link your identities na publicly accessible PC, make sure that before leaving you have deleted all cookies in the browser (for this portal as well as for other institutions used for linking your identities).

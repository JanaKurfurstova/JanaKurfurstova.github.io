How do I get the full text of my document?

Full text not found

We failed to find a link to the full text of the document. Possible reasons are:

  1. Full text is licensed, and none of the participating libraries has subscribed it

  2. Isolated case where the full text exists, but cannot be found in due to inconsistencies in its bibliographic metadata

Full text is freely available without restrictions

The link should bring you to a full text without the necessity of signing in.

Full text is available for institutional users

Registration required. Some libraries allow online or remote registration to access electronic information resources.

  • Log in

  • If you do not have a registration in any library, learn about the possibilities of registration in Czech libraries..

Limited availability

Some institutions could pay for access to certain resources within a limited timeline.
Institutions with limited resource avalability are marked with an asterisk *.

E.g.:

  • Moravian Library *
    (Number of days since publishing until the full text will be made available: 365)

  • National Library of Technology *
    (Number of days since publishing when the full text is available: 500)

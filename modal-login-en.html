You can log in with an identity of any institution listed in "Connect with library's identity" or social network identity from "Alternative login" list (Google, Facebook, LinkedIn, GitHub, ORCID).

Once you sign in with an identity of any participating library, you gain access an overview of your activities in this library, and you will be able to order or reserve publications.

In case you are not registered in any of the connected libraries and logged in through a social network account or ORCID, you will not be able to order or reserve a publication in connected libraries but you can still save your favorite records and searches.

Learn more about registration in participating libraries here.

You can link your library and social network identities in Knihovny.cz (read more).

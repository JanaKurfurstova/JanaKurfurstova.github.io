Domestic Resources

  • Collections of Czech libraries, domestic databases and bibliographies. They also include documents in other languages if they are available in Czech libraries.

  • Most of the documents can be physically borrowed.

International Resources

  • Extensive index of licensed databases, scientific information, and peer-reviewed publications.

  • Most of the documents are accessible on-line. Some are free but in many cases you are required to register in one of the participating libraries.

  • It also contains some documents in Czech.

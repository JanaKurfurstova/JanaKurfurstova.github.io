Why to log in via a participating library?

As a logged-in user you are able to:

  • view your accounts in the connected libraries, including checked-in loans, registration validity, or charges
  • renew your loans, order and reserve publications
  • manage lists of your favorite publications, view your search history and save selected search queries for later reuse

Do you want to borrow apublication or gain access to electronic resources and you are not registered in any of the following libraries?

Register (and pay) online in:

  • Moravian Library in Brno (more info here)

Registration will take a few minutes. After registration you will be able to use all services of the library.

Register remotely in:

  • National Medical Library
  • Academy of Sciences Library
  • Regional Research Library in Liberec (more info here)
  • Municipal Library in Tabor (more info here)

Remote registration consists of more steps than online registration. You can be asked to contact the library via e-mail for further instructions about registration, or the registration fee will have to be paid afterwards (e.g. via bank transfer to the library’s bank account). This depends on the policy of particular library.

Filling this form to gain a publication borrowed by another user makes a reservation of this publication. The publication will be available after it is returned by previous readers (you can see "Your position in queue" below).

Filling this form to have an available publication brought from stock or hold at the particular branch library makes an order of this publication.

The library usually informs you via email that the publication is ready for you.

For further information please check the rules of your library.

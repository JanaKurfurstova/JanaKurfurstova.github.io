Tips for searching in examples

  • Edgar Allan Poe
    multi-word term search results prioritize search term equivalence to author names (in order name surname) and main publication titles.
  • "Poe's Raven" - Domestic and International resources
    search phrase between quotation marks, i. e. Poe's Raven
  • Poe AND Raven - Domestic and International resources
    search terms containing Poe as well as Raven
  • Poe OR Raven - Domestic and International resources
    search terms containing at least one of the words Poe or Raven
  • Poe NOT Raven - Domestic and International resources
    Poe -Raven - Domestic Resources
    search terms containing Poe and not containing Raven
  • poe AND (raven OR (life AND work)) - Domestic and International resources
    you can use parentheses (even nested) to make advanced queries
    searching is not case sensitive and ignores punctation and diacritics
  • +Poe Raven - Domestic resources
    search terms necessarily containing Poe and possibly containing Raven
  • R*ven - Domestic resources (cannot be used in the beginning of the term)
    Rave* - International resources (truncation symbol; cannot be used with ? and #)
    search terms with empty or random string of characters in place of * (you cannot use that in the beginning of the word)
  • R?ven - Domestic and International resources
    search terms begining with R and ending with ven with one character in place of ? (you cannot use that in the beginning of the term)
  • R#ven - International resources
    search terms begining with R and ending with ven with one or zero character in place of # (you cannot use that in the beginning of the term)
  • Raven~ / Raven~0.8 - Domestic resources
    search terms similar to Raven (Haven, Ravel); degree of similarity is defined by setting value from 0 to 1 (default is 0.5)
  • "Poe Raven"~3 - Domestic resources
    Poe N3 Raven - International resources
    proximity search; search terms with at most three words between Poe and Raven (regardless of the order)
  • Poe W3 Raven - International resources
    proximity search; search terms with at most three words between Poe and Raven (in this order)
  • Poe Raven^8 poetry^6 - Domestic resources
    preferential search where the word significanceis given by the value after ^ (a term without ^ has the lowest value)
  • {Po TO Pr} / [2001 TO 2003] - Domestic resources
    range search (terms begining with Po, Pp, Pq and Pr / numbers from 2001 to 2003)

International resources are indexed by the third side - that is why some operators are different.
